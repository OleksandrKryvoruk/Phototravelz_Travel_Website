2.Mudam Museum
Mudam is an art museum in the city of Luxembourg. Located on the territory of the castle Three acorns. Currently, there are about 230 works by more than 100 artists. The museum has various art genres: painting, graphics, photography, multimedia, fashion, design, graphics and sound.

Vianden Castle - Castle Vianden in north-eastern Luxembourg. The building is located on a hill above the town and the river valley. The castle was probably built around the XI century. It was restored and is now available to the general public.

4.Grand Ducal Palace

Grand Ducal Palace is a palace in the city of Luxembourg. There is an official residence where the Grand Duke of Luxembourg carries out the majority of his duties as head of state. Foreign heads of state are on official visits in the palace as guests of the Grand Duke.

5.Bock
By Tristan Schmurr CC2
Casemates du Bock - cape on the corner of the north-eastern historical part of Luxembourg. Being a natural formation, the rocky cliffs of the cape tower above the Alzeta River, which surrounds it from three sides. There in 963 in the year, Count Siegfried built the castle of Lutzelburg. The ruins of the old castle and the huge underground passage system and galleries known as casemates are now an outstanding historical monument of the city.

6.Musée des tramways
Musée des tramways et de bus de la ville de Luxembourg - is dedicated to the history of public transport of this city from the time of the horse to the present day. In the museum's collection there are four original tram cars (two motor and two trailers), a modern replica of a wagon train, two buses and a service car-tower for servicing the contact network. In addition to these full-scale exhibits, the museum's collection includes 22 models of trams and buses at a scale of 1: 8, numerous artifacts (the form of public transport workers, travel tickets, etc.) and documents and about eight thousand photographs.
