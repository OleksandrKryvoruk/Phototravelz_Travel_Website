15.Caucasus
Caucasus is a geopolitical region on the border between Europe and Asia, the Caspian and Black Sea. Center for Tourism and Mountaineering, Alpine skiing in Russia. In the Caucasus, there are almost limitless opportunities for recreation: beautiful landscapes and diverse landscapes, beach resorts and ski slopes, mild climate, unique flora, ancient monuments, medicinal mineral springs, as well as the famous Caucasian cuisine that has become popular far beyond this region. The most popular health-improving region in the Caucasus are the resorts of the Caucasian Mineral Waters: Pyatigorsk, Zheleznovodsk, Essentuki and Kislovodsk. Caucasian ski resorts on infrastructure are inferior to European ones, but local beauty and steep slopes compensate this shortcoming. In this region there are three popular resorts: Elbrus, Dombai and Krasnaya Polyana.

16.Altai
Altai - is a federal object in Russia. Moderately - continental climate, with a short, hot summer and a long, cold winter. High mountain ranges and deep valleys of rivers characteristic for Altai. Altai is a mountain forest country. In the south-west, high-altitude vegetation belts are distinctly revealed: the steppe (up to 350-500 m), forest (up to 2600 m), subalpine and alpine. The most widespread forest belt is the Altaic taiga (Siberian larch, spruce, pine cedar, spruce, pine). A valuable animal of Altai is the deer, whose horns (pantas) are used in medicine. Maralov is bred in specialized farms. To preserve and increase the number of game animals, a large Altai Reserve was created in the area of ​​the Teletskoye Lake.

18.Volga
The Volga is a river in Russia, with a length of 3530 km - the largest river in Europe. In the Volga, there are about 70 fish species, 40 of them are industrial (the most important are Vobla, herring, bream, pike perch, carp, catfish, pike, sturgeon, sterlet). The Volga originates in the Valdai Upland (at an altitude of 229 m), it flows into the Caspian Sea. The mouth lies 28 m below sea level. The total drop is 256 m. The Volga is the world's largest river of internal flow, that is, it does not flow into the world ocean. The Volga Delta is the largest in Europe. The river system of the Volga basin includes 151 thousand watercourses (rivers, streams and temporary watercourses) with a total length of 574 thousand km. The Volga accepts about 200 tributaries.

On the whole, the vegetation in the lake basin mountain-taiga, it has more than 1200 species. Through the influence of the reserve are everywhere there is a great density of various animals. North waters of the sea lies in the Arctic and the southern - in the subarctic climate zone. In the north, a year dominated by polar air masses, and in the south in the summer - moderate air masses.
