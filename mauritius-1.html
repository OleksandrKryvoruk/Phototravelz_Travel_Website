Top 10 places for travel & vacation in Mauritius.

AFRICA > South Africa
1.Black River Gorges National Park
By Adamina CC2
Black River Gorges National Park - Mauritius National Park. Since 1977, part of the park is included in the world network of biosphere reserves as Maccabi-Bel-Ombr, from 2006 the park is nominated for inclusion in the UNESCO World Heritage List. There are 8 rare species of birds in the park: Columba mayeri, Psittacula eques, Coracina typica, Falco punctatus, Collocalia francica, Hypsipetes olivaceus, Zosterops chloronothos, Foudia rubra. There are programs to restore their numbers. About 60 km of tourist routes are laid on the territory of the park, educational programs for tourists are conducted.

2.Blue beach
Blue beach - beautiful beach in the south-east of Mauritius. The beach is great for swimming, diving and relaxation. The underwater world is made up of 72 coral species (some over 800 years old) and 32 species of fish, it can be studied with the help of boats with glass bottoms. The local beach is quiet, beautiful and popular.

3.Flic-en-Flac
By Toutaitanous CC3
Flic-en-Flac is a coastal city in the western part of the island in Mauritius, in the Rivieres-Noire area. The public white beach is one of the longest and most beautiful on the island and its lagoon is protected by surrounding coral reefs. The beach offers a breathtaking view of the Indian Ocean. Flic-en-Flac is also home to some of the most luxurious hotels in Mauritius.

6.Le Morne Brabant
By  Mark Rasdall CC2
Le Morne Brabant is a peninsula on the south-western tip of the island of Mauritius. Over the peninsula to a height of 556 meters the hill and basalt rock rises. Tourist Centre. The area became famous in the XIX century, when in the numerous caves of the hill refuge slaves were found. In 2008, the landscape of the peninsula was included in the UNESCO World Heritage List. Le Morne is one of the best places in the world for riding on waves with a kite, on a windsurf and a paddle board. These types of aquatic activity are widely represented in this part of Mauritius.

10.Ganga Talao
By carrotmadman6 CC2
Ganga Talao is a crater lake located in a secluded mountainous area, in the district of Savan Mauritius. Grand Bassin is at an altitude of 549 meters above sea level. The lake is considered the most holy Hindu place in Mauritius. According to legend, a person who finds himself on a lakeside island, soon dies.
