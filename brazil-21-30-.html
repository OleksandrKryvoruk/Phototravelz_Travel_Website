21.Fernando de Noronha
Fernando de Noronha is an archipelago in the Atlantic Ocean, 360 km off the coast of Brazil. Administratively, it is part of the Brazilian state of Pernambuco. All islands are of volcanic origin and represent the summits of seamounts, protruding from the sea. In 2001, UNESCO included the island of Fernando de Noronha and the atoll of Rokas to the World Heritage List. Fernando de Noronha is the best place in Brazil for scuba diving. The warm sea allows you to dive to a depth of 25 to 40 meters, where there is a rich variety of underwater flora and fauna.

22.Blumenau
Blumenau is a city in the south of Brazil. The city was founded on September 2, 1850 by the German chemist and business manager Herman Bruno Otto Blumenau. The city is in a valley and is surrounded by forests and hills. Every October in the city is Oktoberfest from Blumenau - this festival is visited by more than 1 million tourists. There are also several sightseeing routes and each city hosts a festival of the municipality. The common language of communication is German, the second is Italian.

23.Itacaré
Itacare is a municipality in Brazil, part of the state of Bahia. The place is rich in beaches, as well as cultural attractions. The city is popular with surfers, and there are several events related to this sport, such as the Eco Surf Festival under the patronage of Secretaria to Turismo do Estado (State Secretariat for Tourism) or the World Surfing Championship organized by the World Surfing League (WSL). The city is one of the main tourist centers of the southern coast of Bahia.

24.Angra dos Reis
Angra dos Reis is a municipality in the south of Rio de Janeiro. Includes about 365 islands and more than 2,000 beaches with beautiful nature. There are also several waterfalls. Along the coast stretches a mountain chain, drowning in the green of a dense Atlantic forest. This resort likes to visit celebrities: rock stars and representatives of bohemia. And some popular Brazilian TV shows are broadcast from the most popular beaches of the coast.

25.Buzios
By karendesuyo CC2
Buzios is a municipality in Brazil, part of the state of Rio de Janeiro. An integral part of the Baixadas-Litoraaneas region. Armação dos Búzios, with its strong winds, is ideal for yachting and free flight. It is a city in which there are many different cultures and a large number of foreigners. The tourist zone is formed by a peninsula, in which there are 8 beaches. Today the city is visited by tourists from all over the world, some call it the Brazilian Saint-Tropez.

26.Porto de Galinhas
By Natália Marinho CC2
Porto de Galinhas is a city and beach in the municipality of Ipozuca in Pernambuco, Brazil. Located 60 kilometers (37 miles) south of the state capital, Recife. Together with the beaches of Pontal de Maracaype and Muro Alto, they form a line of beaches about 10 km in length, which are recognized as natural pools that form in the sea, and coral reefs with a wide variety of fish. Port de Galinhas is a popular tourist destination.

27.Vila Velha
By Vinicius Depizzol CC2
Vila Velha - a city on the Atlantic coast in Brazil. The coastal town line is 30 km long and almost all presented beaches. The main beaches are: Barra do Jucu (take the championship surfing), Praia da Barrinha, Praia da Concha (calm beach), Praia da Costa (the most popular beach town, near a lot of hotels and restaurants), Praia do Ribeiro, Prainha. The town is worth visiting the Church of the Convento da Penha (1558), Сave Fray Pedro Palacios, Forte de San - Francisco Xavier de piratininga, an obelisk in Vasco Fernandes Coutinho, Railway Museum.

28.Itajaí
Itajai is a city in Brazil. The city has many beautiful beaches and hosts a fishing festival. In the city there are more than 10 professional theatrical groups, as well as many cultural sites of German, Italian, Portuguese immigrants. Marejad is a Portuguese holiday (on the recognized date of the first Portuguese landing on the coast of Brazil), cooking, folklore and dancing. Marejad is held annually in October. It is the largest Portuguese and fish festival in Brazil.
Where to stay Weather Things to do in Itajai: Sightseeing Tickets & Passes Theme Parks Tours & Sightseeing
 

29.Paraty
Paraty - the area near Rio de Janeiro. The city has many monuments of the Portuguese colonial culture, and he is surrounded by tropical forests 80% of which include nature reserves, national parks, ecological stations. Architectural monuments include the Capela de Santa Rita, Capela de Nossa Senhora das Dores, Capela da Generosa, Igreja de Nossa Senhora da Penha, Forte Defensor, Forte Patitiba all the buildings 17 and 18 century. Also held many musical and cultural events. Also in the city there are many beaches that offer diving and boat trips.
Where to stay Weather Things to do in Paraty: Cruises, Sailing & Water Tours Cultural & Theme Tours Food, Wine & Nightlife Outdoor Activities Tours & Sightseeing Walking & Biking Tours Water Sports
 

30.Praia de Pipa
By Tainá Lossëhelin CC2
Praia de Pipa - the famous beach in the municipality of Tibau do Sul, is 85 km from Natal, the capital of the state of Rio Grande do Norte, Brazil. Known for having one of the busiest nightlife in the state, it has a large number of hotels, hotels, hostels, restaurants, bars, discos. It is the main seaside resort on the southern coast of the state, which includes beaches: Pont Madeiro and Love Beach. The beach also hosts the annual Pipa Gastronomic Festival. Pipa attracts many surfers and kitesurfers.
