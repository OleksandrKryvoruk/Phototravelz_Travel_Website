13.The Blue Mosque
The Blue Mosque is a historical mosque in Istanbul, Turkey. The Blue Mosque is the main tourist attraction of the city. There are six minarets: four, as usual, on the sides, and two slightly less high ones - on the outer corners. It is considered an outstanding example of Islamic and world architecture. The mosque is located on the shores of the Sea of Marmara, in the historical center of Istanbul in the Sultanahmet area, opposite the Aya Sofia Museum.

14.Dolmabahçe Palace
Dolmabahçe Palace - the palace of the Ottoman Sultans on the European side of the Bosphorus in Istanbul in the border areas of Beşiktaş and Kabataş. Baroque Palace, able to compete with European sophistication in the residences of the XVIII century. On Decoration Crystal stairs and other interior took 14 tons of gold. Among the main attractions - donated by Queen Victoria with Bohemian glass chandelier weighing almost 5 tons, and paintings by Ivan Aivazovsky, which the artist was commissioned by Sultan. Now the palace is a museum. Inspect the interior of the palace is only possible as part of the tour.

15.Fethiye
Fethiye - district and the resort town in south-western Turkey. The city is located at the foot of the mountains covered with pine and cedar lesami. In 40 kilometers from the town is Dalaman Airport. In Fethiye, which is called "England in Miniature" home to more than 7,000 British families, the resort also annually receives more than 600,000 tourists from England. On the territory of the city in ancient times there was a city Telmessos. The city was very famous, and was the center of the prophecies, dedicated to Apollo. In the city Museum, which presents a collection of archaeological finds and ethnological artefacts.

16.Taurus Mountains
Taurus Mountains - the southern coastal mountains on the territory of modern Turkey. Taurus stretch along the Turkish coast of the Mediterranean Sea from the Aegean Sea to the headwaters of the Euphrates River. They form a continuous series of wooded mountain ranges, crossed by numerous river valleys. In the eastern part of Cilicia top of them reach a height of 3000 to 3500 m, and further west -. From 2000 to 3000 m In addition to hiking and mountaineering, there are two ski resorts in the mountain range, one for «Davras» about 25 km (16 miles) from the two nearest cities Egirdir and Istanbul, the second Saklikent which is 40 km (25 miles) from the city of Antalya.

Streams of water are cut off from a cliff 40 meters high into the sea, forming a unique look that is one of the cities and provinces Antali. Up Duden is a business card of 14 km above the mouth. At the waterfall a lot of greenery and located a number of restaurants and cafes, so the waterfall is one of the favorite places not only tourists but also residents of Antalya.
