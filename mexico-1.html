1.Cancún
Cancun is a large resort town on the Yucatán Peninsula, Mexico. It is included in the top 10 resorts of the world. The resort is located on a sandy spit, reminiscent of the figure "7", a width of 400 m and a length of 30 km. The upper part of the spit overlooks the Bay of Women, where the water is almost always calm, the lower part - to the open sea with an impressive surf. On its entire length are hotel complexes, entertainment and shopping centers, discos, marine entertainment bars, restaurants, cafes and the like. Few resorts can compare with Cancun for the beauty and purity of the sea, proximity to the world famous archaeological zones, nature reserves and, of course, the purity of white beaches. The main attractions include: Koba (ancient structures and caves), Sak Aktun (underground rivers) and the ruins of El Ray.

2.Acapulco
Acapulco - tourist and port city on the Pacific coast. The oldest and most famous resort center of Mexico. One of the most favorite places of rest of celebrities. The main attraction of the city is its active nightlife, which takes place in bars, restaurants, nightclubs and the beach areas. In Acapulco there are a number of beautiful sandy beaches and high rock for fans to jump into the water from a height. You can also visit the zoo, water park, botanical garden, the museum Casa de la Máscara, bullring and a festival of French culture.

3.Cozumel
Cozumel - an island and a popular resort in the Caribbean in Mexico. Near the island is the world's second largest coral reef (700 km). Vegetation is represented by mango forests. The main type of occupation of the island is diving. There are a number of certified centers are professionally involved in teaching scuba diving. You can also take a ride on a catamaran, glass bottom boat, immersed in a mini submarine and go sport fishing.

4.Cabo San Lucas
Cabo San Lucas - one of the most popular seaside resorts in Mexico, located in the extreme south of the peninsula of Baja California and the state of Baja California Sur. Cabo is on the list of the top 5 places to visit in Mexico. The place is famous for its beaches, underwater world with amazing flora and fauna, numerous resorts along the coast and spa centers. In winter, whale migration can be observed here. Cabo San Lucas is a port for tourist cruises, making sea voyages. Cabo San Lucas is the second in the world by the most expensive ports.

5.Ixtapa
 By Krystal International vacation club CC2
Ixtapa is a settlement in Mexico, in the state of Guerrero. Ixtapa is the second most important tourist destination in the state and one of the most important in Mexico, has a marina 2 km away. Istapa is an important western resort of Mexico: cruise ships often stop here. Popular beach holidays, scuba diving, water activities. The city has private villas, restaurants, shops, a beach club and a tennis center, as well as an 18-hole golf course. Ixtapa offers many hotels, more than 5,000 hotel rooms in Ixtapa are available for tourists.

6.Playa del Carmen
 By Andrewarchy CC2
Playa del Carmen is the city and administrative center of the municipality of Solidaridad in the Mexican state of Quintana Roo. The city is located in the middle of the tourist zone of the Riviera Maya on the Caribbean coast. The third largest city in the state after Cancun and Chetumal. Near the Playa del Carmen are several attractive objects for tourists: Shkaret, an ecological theme park dedicated to the Mayan culture. Cozumel, an island located opposite Playa del Carmen, to which only 45 minutes by ferry. Cancun, the famous resort town. Tulum, located on the shore of an archaeological site belonging to the Maya tribe. Sian-Kaan, Biosphere Reserve. Sak Aktun, a system of underground rivers. Iksplor, amusement park.

8.Chichén Itzá
Chichen Itza - the holy city of the Mayan culture in the north of the Yucatan Peninsula, Mexico. Included in the list of the new 7 wonders of the world. In its heyday the city was one of the most powerful centers of the Mayan culture. The major architectural monuments include the Temple of Kukulkan (pyramid 24 meters high), the Sacred Cenote (natural well depth of 50 meters), Temple of Warriors in Chichen Itza, the Mayan statues of deities. Included in the list of World Cultural Heritage by UNESCO.

9.Teotihuacán
Teotihuacan is an abandoned city, in the valley of Mexico, in which the largest pyramids of Mesoamerica are located (the pyramid of the Sun is the third highest in the world after the pyramid of Cheops in Egypt and the Great Pyramid in Cholula in Mexico). Scientists consider it to be the oldest city in the western hemisphere. The central part of the city Square Citadel - can accommodate up to 100 thousand people. The area is surrounded by four garden pyramids. They are adjoined by the Southern and Northern palaces. In addition to pyramidal structures, Teotihuacan is famous for its large complex of structures (the so-called "Street of the Dead") and bright and well-preserved frescoes.

10.Mexico City
Mexico City is the capital and largest city of Mexico, the center of the Federal District of the Federal District. The population is 8.8 million people, the largest Spanish-speaking city in the world. Within the agglomeration 20 million people live. According to estimates, the capital has more than 100 museums and 10 archaeological parks. In Mexico City, there are many parks and forests, some of them protected nature reserves, some of the largest and most important parks and forests located within the city. In the city there are many monuments and unique buildings (there are more than 1400 in Mexico). There are also 10 archaeological parks on the territory of Mexico City.
