21.Kangaroo Island
Kangaroo Island - the third largest island of Australia. Over 30% of the island's national parks: Flinders Chase National Park, Sealbay Reserve, Cape Gentheaume Wilderness, Cape Bouguer Wilderness. Island shakes its wildlife: possums, koalas, rabbits, foxes, kangaroos, Yechida, flying mouses, frogs and many others. SeaWorld island is also rich in diversity. On the beaches of the island are many deep caves which passes many interesting excursions.

22.Darwin
By Ken Hodge CC2
Darwin is a city in Australia, the capital of the Northern Territory. It is located on the shore of the Timor Sea. Darwin grew up from a small port in one of the most modern cities in Australia. The city has many miles of wide untouched beaches, including the beach of Casuarina and the famous Mindil beach, where the markets of Mindil Beach are located. Fishing is one of Darwin's leisure activities. Visitors from all over the world flock to the city to catch precious barramundi. In Darwin there are extensive parks and gardens. These include the George Brown Darwin Botanical Gardens, the Eastern Reserve, Charles Darwin National Park, the Kusuarina Coastal Reserve.

23.Hobart
Hobart is the capital and largest city of the Australian state of Tasmania. The city is located at the foot of the Wellington mountain, whose height is 1271 meters. Worth visiting: Salamanca, Battery Point, Australian Antarctic Department Headquarters, Tasmanian Maritime Museum, Royal Botanic Gardens of Tasmania, Tasmanian Museum and Art Gallery, Major national and international music events are usually held at the Derwent entertainment center or at the Casino. Hobart is known around the world among sailing communities as the end of the Sydney race. The annual tulip festival at the Royal Botanic Gardens of Tasmania is a popular spring holiday.

24.Curl Curl
Curl Curl-Beach, one of the best surf beaches in Sydney, Australia. The beach has its own surfing and life saving clubs, cafes, kiosks and reaches about 1.2 km. The northern part has a shallow lagoon, where you can engage in the construction of figures in the sand, visit parks and playgrounds, perfect for children. Also on the beach you can play football, netball, acting Amateur Swimming Club, which holds events every weekend. Near the beach there is a bowling club which hosts world-class tournaments.

25.Freycinet National Park
By Bjørn Christian Tørrissen CC3
Freycinet National Park - located on the coast of Tasmania (Australia), 125 km north-east of Hobart. Freycineta Park consists of fragments of the wild, strong coastline of Tasmania, and there is a bay of vineyards in it. Among the unique features of the park are pink and red granite formations and granite peaks formed in a line called "Dangers". Freycinetta National Park was founded in 1916, is the oldest park in Tasmania. You can stay here both in the camping and in the luxurious hotel. In addition to enjoying the beauty of these places, you can do fishing, boating, hiking, mountaineering.

26.Kakadu National Park
By Ian Diversi CC2
Kakadu National Park is one of the most beautiful parks in Australia and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The park has the world's largest collection of rock carvings. On the territory of the park there are several beautiful waterfalls and canyons, there are interesting excursions. It is better not to swim in the local waters as they swarm with crocodiles. In the Kakadu National Park, more than two hundred species of endemic plants, including palms, ficus and eucalyptus. From the animals in the reserve there are koala, kangaroos, wombats, from birds - cassowaries, parrots, lyrebirds and others.
Where to stay Weather Things to do in Kakadu National Park: Day Trips & Excursions Multi-day & Extended Tours

27.Uluru
Uluru - formed about 680 million years ago in Australia, the sacred mountain of Australian Aborigines, one of Australia's most famous landmarks. The mountain is 863 meters high and is part of the UNESCO World Heritage Site. In the national park in whose territory the mountain is located there is a cultural center where you can learn the history and legend of the mountain. Near the mountain there is a tourist and hotel center, camping in the park is prohibited. Near Uluru there is another famous complex - Kata Tjuta. It consists of several dark red mountains of round shape, the highest of which reaches 546 m.

28.Great Ocean Rd
Great Ocean Road - is 243 km along the Australian coast, which is considered one of the most beautiful places in Australia. Each year, this road has more than 7 million. Tourists, it is a national monument of historical and cultural significance and is part of the national heritage list. Every year there is held the Olympic marathon length of 45 km. The road has a good infrastructure which has been spent over 500 million Australian dollars.

29.Royal National Park
By Jenny Mealing CC2
Royal National Park is a national park in New South Wales, Australia. The park is the second oldest national park after Yellowstone and the first protected area to which the expression "national park" itself is used. Today the territory of the national park occupies more than 15 thousand hectares. Many species of flora and fauna grow in it, some of them are endangered. From the north, the park borders on South Sydney and the Bay of Port Hacking, from the east its territory is washed by the waters of the Tasman Sea. There are several campsites in Bundeen, North Aire and Providence Point. In the park there are several hiking trails, camels and picnics. Mountain bikes are also allowed on several hiking trails in the park.

30.Whitsunday Island Reef
Whitsunday Islands consist of 74 islands in the Coral Sea to the northeast of Queensland in Australia and have the status of a national park. The islands are located 35 nautical miles from the Great Barrier Reef. The islands are the tops of a volcano that are under water. The territory of the park is inhabited by many sea birds, and you can also see humpback whales. Camping is allowed on the islands, but the place needs to be reserved in advance. Islands of Whitsunday in Australia - one of the popular places among lovers of scuba diving.
