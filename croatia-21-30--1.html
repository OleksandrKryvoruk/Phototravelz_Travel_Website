21.Krka National Park
Krka National Park - national park in Croatia. Located in the middle of Dalmatia, in the Krka River Valley, between the towns of Knin, Sibenik. On the river within the National Park, seven large cascades of waterfalls with a total height difference of 242 m. The park's flora includes more than 860 species, including several endemic species. In the waters of the Krka there are 18 species of fish, 10 of which are endemic. A variety of birds inhabit the park. Major attractions: Seven beautiful cascades: Bilushicha Beech - 22 meters, Brlyan - 15 meters, Manoylovach - drop 60 meters, Roshnyak - 8 meters, Miljacka Slap - 24 meters, Powder Slap - 22.5 meters, Skradinski Beech - 46 meters. Visovac Franciscan Monastery - located on an island Visovac among Krky. It founded in the XIV century Augustinian. Serbian Orthodox monastery Krka - was founded in the XIV century. Ethnographic Museum - next to the waterfall Skradinski Buk. Among other things, water mills and exhibited a kind of "washing machine" working on the waterfall energy.

22.Kornati National park
Kornati National park - Croatian archipelago to the south of Zadar. It consists of about 147 islands. Interesting places: Crowns - a geological phenomenon that is a rock, "growing" directly from the sea. They reach up to 100 meters, both in the underwater part and in the above water. The ascent to the Crown is forbidden. Remains of Illyrian settlements are located on several islands of the archipelago. The fortress of Turet is on the island of Kornat. The oldest building of the Byzantine era on the archipelago. It dates back to the VI century.

23.Samobor
Samobor - city in Croatia. One of the oldest and most popular sightseeing destinations in Croatia. Architectural monuments: the castle ruins Samobor Church St. Anastasia XVII century, the Church of St. Michael the XVI - XVII century, the Church of the Assumption of the Blessed Virgin Mary, Franciscan monastery, XVIII century mansion Ferda Livadicha XVII century. Events in town: Samobor Carnival (between winter and spring), Samobor Music Festival, salami, Oldtimer Rally (July), Days of bread and wine.

24.Lovrijenac
Lovrijenac - fort in the fortification strip of Dubrovnik, Croatia outside the city walls. Legend has it that the fort was built in the early 11th century, for three months. The walls on land and at sea have a thickness of 4-12 m, the ceiling is 1.5 m. On the arch of the main entrance to the fortress there is the slogan "Non Bene Pro Toto Libertas Venditur Auro" "Without money in the world we sell our freedom", which later became the motto the city of Dubrovnik.

25.Motovun
Motovun - a town located on the Croatian peninsula of Istria, 30 km from the Adriatic sea. City located on a hill 277 m in height, at the foot of which flows the river Peace. In the valley of Motovun there are no factories, only the wine lands and farms, making this valley a local center for eco-tourism. The main souvenir of this town are the local wines and brandies and truffles. The entrance to the town of Motovun is the goal. Vintage inner gate, which adorn the Lions Brand, are the market square, to St. Stephen's Church. The building was designed by renowned architect Andrea Palladio. In recent years, in July, in order to attract tourists and promote the cultural life of the city in the Motovun Film Festival is held, and the truffle festival held in October. Between the town of Motovun and Oprtalj is massive forest reserve "Motovunsky forest" - the only floodplain forest of the Mediterranean that has remained intact.

27.Varaždin
Varazdin - city in north-eastern Croatia. The city is known for its Baroque architecture. Varazdin - cultural and educational center of the country. The city has a theater, the City Museum (located in the old town), its subsidiary - Entomological Museum (in the Herzer palace). Varazdin is famous for its "Evening of Baroque Music", which collect the performers and participants from many countries. Concerts are held in the ancient churches and castles of the city. One of the most famous annual events, attracting thousands of visitors, is the "Shpantsirfest" - festival of street performers. From well-known cultural events worth mentioning Trash Film Festival - International Film Festival of militants little budget and related genres. Cultural and historical attraction Varazdin undoubtedly Varazhdinska Civil Guard, which carries every Saturday to guard Town Hall, participates in ceremonies at major city and county anniversaries and the like. At 40 kilometers from the town stands the beautiful Gothic castle Trakošćan.

28.Osijek
Osijek - a town in the eastern part of Croatia. Osijek - the fourth largest city in Croatia after Zagreb, Split and Rijeka. Osijek is a popular item of domestic tourism because of its historical and cultural wealth. The most important monuments and places of interest is the central city of Osijek square Ante Starcevic, the fortress of the XVIII century, the promenade along the Drava embankment, suspended footbridge over the river sheep. In Osijek preserved baroque castle-fortress of the XVIII century. The complex - the church of St. Michael. The city is interesting for its architecture - at the center of many Baroque buildings, Art Nouveau, Art Nouveau. National Monuments in Osijek as a city park of King Petar Kreshemir IV and the Tomislav Park early XX century. The city is one of the few Croatian zoological, and the largest in the country - Osietsky Zoo. Among the city's museums - Archaeological and Art Gallery.

Premantura - a small town on the Pula Riviera, Croatia. The resort is located in the central part of the peninsula Kamenjak. The sea near the village is shallow and the beaches are very beautiful. The resort offers water sports, cycling, underground caves, forest park Monte Ghiro, Dont Reserve Kamenjak, open-air museum Gea.
