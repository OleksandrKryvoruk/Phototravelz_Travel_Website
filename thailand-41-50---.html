41.Nong Nooch Tropical Botanical Garden
Nong Nooch Tropical Botanical Garden - a tropical botanical garden near Pattaya, Thailand. Garden consists of several thematic gardens for a variety of species of plants and animals. The garden is home to elephants, which show tourists show program. Types of themed gardens: French Garden, Butterfly Garden, Cactus Garden, Orchid Garden, Silver Garden, Bonsai Garden, Stonehenge garden. Nong Nooch Tropical Botanical Garden is open daily from 09:00 to 17:00.

42.Phayao
Phayao is the capital of the province of Phayao, Thailand. The city is in the western part of the province, on the watershed of the Mekong and Chauphraya rivers, on the eastern shore of the lake of the same name. The city was built as a semi-independent city in the 12th century. Interesting places: Kwan Phayao is the largest freshwater lake in the north of Thailand, Wat Si Khom Kham is a Buddhist temple, Doi Phu Nang National Park, Mae Puem National Park, Phu Sang National Park.
Where to stay Weather

43.Amnat Charoen
Amnat Charoen - the city is located in the province of Amnat Charoen in the north-east of Thailand. Interesting places: Wat Mongkhon Ming Mueang Buddhist temple, Wat Tham San Phet Buddhist temple complex with several caves, Phuttha Utthayan - Buddhist park, also serves as a recreation area. The enigmatic, unique and virgin nature of Amnat Charena is simply mesmerizing. Beautiful clean waterfalls bring down their waters between the emerald-green thickets. The theme for travel can be the exploration of this amazing nature and enjoyment of all the beauty of the tropical landscape.
Where to stay Weather
 

44.Ubon Ratchathani
Ubon Ratchathani - a city in the eastern part of Thailand, on the plain of Korat, the river Mun. Ubon candle famous summer festival. Each monastery has a platform on wheels, which are mounted on giant sculptures on religious themes of wax. During the celebration, each monastery exports decorated platform on the streets for a festive carnival. In October festival spend Wack Ok Phansa during which competitions are held on the illuminated boats along the Moon River. The outstanding construction is the golden pagoda Wat Nong Bua.
Where to stay Weather
 

45.Ko Phayam
Ko Phayam - considered the most beautiful and least known islands in Thailand. It is the second largest island in the Andaman Sea off the coast of Ranong province. The island has no high-grade roads suitable for motor vehicles, the primary mode of transportation - mopeds and motorcycles. Tourists can rent a moped on the pier upon arrival. Koh Payam two main beach of Ao Yai - the longest beach, suitable for surfing, and Ao Kao Kwai (Buffalo Bay) - a more intimate beach for a relaxing holiday.
Where to stay Weather
 

46.Tarutao National Park
By Toey19863 CC4
Tarutao National Park - consists of 51 islands located in the Andaman Sea, near the coast of the province of Satun in the south of Thailand. The park consists of two island groups: Tarutau and Butang Island (Adang-Ravi), which are scattered from 20 to 70 kilometers from the mainland. The waters of the Tarutao National Park are in fact home to a variety of colorful corals and other reef inhabitants. There are more than 100 species of birds, such as eagles, osprey, red kites, rhinoceroses, herons. Worth visiting: Waterfall Lu-Du in Son Bay, Crocodile Cave, Pantamarcaka Bay, Son Bay Bay.
Where to stay Weather
47.Khon Kaen
Khon Kaen - is the capital of the eponymous district in Thailand. It is one of the most important economic, cultural, educational and tourist centers in the region. The city has a 5 star hotel, Khon Kaen National Museum - some artifacts around 5000 years. Bueng Kaen Nakhon - Lake in the southeastern part of the city, is a local recreation area. Local festivals: Khon Kaen International Marathon - in late January / early February, Agricultural Fair - the end of January / beginning of February, Silk Festival - end of November / beginning of December, Thai-German Festival at the university.
Where to stay Weather
 

48.Jomtien Beach
By Richard Barton CC2
Jomtien Beach on road signs and maps is often written by Chom Tian, ​​the district of the city and a large beach located in the south of Pattaya (Thailand). Jomtien is located about 3 km from the center of Pattaya and there are many hotels on the beach line, multi-storey condominiums and restaurants. Along the entire coastline is the beach, which is one of the most popular in Pattaya. There are regular competitions in beach and sea sports. Jomtien is a quieter and quieter area of ​​Pattaya relative to the center. Also in the area is the famous gay beach of Dongtan, the only one in Pattaya.

49.Khwae Yai
Khwae Yai - a river in north-western Thailand. He received worldwide fame through the distorted name Kwai movie "Bridge on the River Kwai". Daily fluctuations in the water level in the river reach one and a half meters. On the banks of the river as the shooting took place such famous films as "The Deer Hunter." Khve River - a popular destination of tourism. On the bank of the river is elephant farm, various hotels, including houseboats, tourists also float down the river on rafts and barges. Trips along the river includes a visit to the railway and the thermal springs, located near the river.
Where to stay Weather
 

50.Rama VIII Bridge
Rama VIII Bridge - is a cable bridge located in the Thai capital of Bangkok. The bridge consists of one 160-meter pylon about 1/3 of the length of the bridge from the north-western end of the bridge. The pylon is crowned with a gilded sculpture of a stylized flame. On the pylon the four-lane road hangs on diagonally elongated gilded ropes, which are illuminated at night by headlights. Including access roads, the bridge is 2.45 km long, the main bridge is 475 m long and the largest span is 300 m. The bridge became the main tourist attraction and is located in a new series of banknotes of 20 baht.
