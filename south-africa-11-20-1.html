11.Kirstenbosch National Botanical Garden
Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens - Botanical Gardens near Cape Town, South Africa. It occupies an area of ​​about 530 hectares. In the park there are about 7000 species of plants, some of which are rare or endangered. The park is located near Table Mountain. Included in the UNESCO World Heritage List. The park was created suspended walkway height of 12 m and 427 m long with special viewing platforms.

20.Table Mountain
Table Mountain is one of the main attractions of Cape Town, South Africa. Included in the list of the seven new wonders of nature. The height of 1085 m, the top of the mountain has the shape of a plateau. The mountain slopes are steep and covered with forests. You can find rare species of plants and animals. From the mountain cableway held. To lift up the mountain there are a few trails are also popular hiking up the mountain. Near the mountains you can find porcupines, mongooses, tortoises, lizards and many other animals.
