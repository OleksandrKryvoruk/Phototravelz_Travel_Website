1.Sydney
Sydney - Australia's largest city. The most famous attractions of the city include the Opera House, Harbour Bridge, Royal Botanic Gardens, Taronga Zoo, Port Jackson, Sydney Aquarium, the Museum of Modern Art, the Australian Museum, Sydney Observatory, Luna park, Bondi Beach. The city hosts a lot of festivals Chinese New Year, Mercedes Australian Fashion Week, sea of ​​sculpture - in the spring, Australia Day, Flickerfest, Mardi Gras (sex minorities Festival) - in the summer, Greek Festival, Royal Easter Show, Sydney Morning Herald - autumn, the Australian International music festival, International Boat Show - in the winter.

2.Melbourne
Melbourne is Australia's second largest city. The city museum is the largest in the entire southern hemisphere. The Victoria Museum consists of departments of zoology, geology, anthropology, as well as a gallery with collections of paintings and photographs. In summer, jazz festivals are held in city parks. Each January there are tennis tournament Australian Open, as well as race Formula 1. Also in the city there are very beautiful underwater aquariums. Worth visiting: Victoria Parliament House, Royal Exhibition Center, Crown Casino, Melbourne Aquarium, Victoria National Gallery, Eureka Tower.

3.Perth
Perth is one of the largest cities of Australia. The city has a cultural district, which consists of two galleries of the Art Gallery of Western Australia, the Institute of Contemporary Art, Museum of History, the Maritime Museum, Library and Archives of the State of Western Australia. In the concert hall of the city you can see theater performances, ballets and listen to classical music concerts. Also in Perth is an international arts festival including music, film, theater, literature, visual arts and dance. The city has a zoo, aquarium and botanical garden of 18 hectares, which brings together more than 2 thousand species of plants and birds. On the coast, passes Cottesloe beach Sculpture Festival.

4.Brisbane
Brisbane city is located on the shore of the river of the same name, is one of the best places to live in Australia. The city is known for its sanctuary Lone Pine Koala Sanctuary, which is one of the ten best zoos in the world. In Brisbane there is an area where there are many museums, galleries and exhibition halls, it is called Brisbane Cultural Precinct. Also in the city there is a wonderful opportunity to ride on the ferries CityFerry and CityCat, which are the business card of the city. Developed scuba diving, including diving to artificial and natural reefs.

5.Cairns
By WANG-HSIN PEI CC2
Cairns is a city in the northeast of the Australian state of Queensland. 40 km from Cairns in the Coral Sea begins the Great Barrier Reef. In terms of attendance by foreign tourists, Cairns is the fourth Australian region, after Sydney, Melbourne and Brisbane. For foreign tourists in this area of ​​greatest interest are the Great Barrier Reef, the Wet Tropics of Queensland and the Atherton Plateau, as well as the tropical climate and sandy beaches of the Coral Sea. The tourist infrastructure of the city includes a large number of hotels, holiday homes, beaches, cafes and restaurants. The beaches are located north of the city center. In all there are about 10 of them.

6.Gold Coast
Gold Coast is a city in the southeast of the Australian state of Queensland. The climate is subtropical, with 287 sunny days per year. The average summer temperatures are 19-29 C and winter temperatures are 9-21 C. The length of the beaches of the city is about 70 km with an average water temperature of 22 ° C. The main branch of the economy is tourism. There are large markets, and many boutiques. In the city there are more than 500 restaurants and cafes, night clubs, hotels, casinos and others. The rainforest is rich in wildlife. In the northern areas of the city there are large farms of sugar cane.

7.Fremantle
Fremantle is a port city with many cafes, restaurants, parks and breweries in Australia. Local restaurants focus on Asian and Italian cuisine, as well as on seafood dishes. By the way, every year there is a festival for lovers of seafood. In the city there are annual races on yachts Club Marine Fremantle. The city has its own football and cricket clubs. On the territory of Fremantle there are 5 well-equipped beaches where you can do water sports and fishing. Fremantle is famous for many architectural landmarks, well preserved, and form a unity with the urban landscapes. First of all, these are colonial buildings built by prisoners, the old pier and port, local prisons.

8.Great Barrier Reef
Great Barrier Reef - the world's largest coral reef, which consists of nearly 3 thousand reefs and 900 islands and stretches 2,300 km, in Australia. It belongs to the UNESCO World Heritage site and is the largest structure made by animals. Reef will be very interesting for divers because the form that opens a stunning underwater. Inspect the reef as you can with the help of a variety of excursions on a glass bottom boat and underwater observatories. From the air the reef you can see the advantage of helicopter tours.

By Jason Tong CC2
Canberra - is the capital of the Commonwealth of Australia. Sightseeing: Black Mountain. Wooded mountain 812 m, Telstra tower - 195 m tower, National Botanical Garden, Parliament House, Old Parliament House, Australian National Gallery, High Court, National Library, National Science and Technology Center, Australian War Memorial, Australian National Botanical Garden. 10.Sydney Opera House
Sydney Opera House - one of the most famous and easily known buildings in the world, which is the symbol of Sydney and one of the main attractions of Australia. It includes 900 rooms, in particular the Concert Hall, the Opera House, the Drama and Chamber Theaters, 4 restaurants and the Reception Hall. The building is located in the Sydney harbor at Cape Bennelong Point and covers an area of 2.2 hectares. In 2007 the building of the theater was nominated in the project Seven new wonders of the world and got into the semifinal. The Opera House was established on June 28, 2007 as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
