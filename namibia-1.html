Top 10 places for travel & vacation in Namibia.

AFRICA > South Africa
1.Windhoek
Windhoek - the capital and largest city in Namibia. The main attractions include a zoo, a botanical garden, the Lutheran Church, the German fort, three castles in the Gothic Revival style, National Museum, National Gallery of Art.

2.Swakopmund
Swakopmund - a city in the center of the Atlantic coast of Namibia. Due to cold currents temperature in all year from 15 to 25 degrees Celsius. Local travel agencies offer: Race through the desert, boat trips, hang-gliding. Local cuisine is German dishes. The main attractions include the Skeleton Coast, Mine Rёssing (the largest uranium mine in the world), City Museum, Swakopmund, lunar landscape, Namib Desert.

3.Dead Vlei
Deadvlei - dead land within the Namib-Naukluft Park in Namibia. The park is 4 largest in the world. Its length along the Atlantic coast is nearly 1,600 kilometers. In the middle of the desert is a plateau and dunes which reach heights of several hundred meters. In the park you can see the lunar landscapes and the dead 550 year old trees.

4.Fish River Canyon
Fish River Canyon - the largest canyon in Africa. Located in the south of Namibia. It is the second most visited place in Namibia. The length of the canyon of 160 km and a depth of almost 550 meters. At the bottom of the canyon flowing river Fish, but most of the year it is in a dried state. Canyon began to form another 60 million years ago.

5.Kalahari
Kalahari Red Dunes Lodge - a private reserve that offers trips for families and small groups of people in Namibia. The resort has a restaurant, bar, Lange, pool, fireplace. Effective buffet, and the cuisine is made up of European and African dishes. All rooms are well equipped with: mosquito nets, air conditioning, Wi-Fi, private terrace, air conditioning. The reserve offers leisure, excursions by 4x4 cars deserts and savannas.

6.Etosha National Park
Etosha National Park - one of the largest national parks in Namibia. The park has its salt marshes, which are called Etosha Pan. Due to the high concentration of salts of flora and fauna here are very poor. In the south, the park has a dolomitic hills Ondundozonananandana. In the park live 114 species of animals, 340 species of birds, 16 species of amphibians, 110 species of reptiles and 49 species of fish. The park has camping: Dolomite Camp, Halali, Namutoni, Okaukuejo, Onkoshi.

7.Sesriem
Sesriem - a small town in the Namib desert. One of the main access points to the National Park Namib-Naukluft. Sesriem offers camping and flying on balloons. Also nearby is the eponymous canyon depth of 30 m.

8.Windhoek Zoo
Zoo Park in the capital of Namibia. Located in the central area of ​​Windhoek. It has a café and a playground. In 1961 there found the remains of elephants, which are more than 5000 years.

9.Spitzkoppe
Spitzkoppe - a group of granite peaks that are more than 120 million years in Namibia. The highest point is 1784 m. In the mountains you can see ancient rock paintings of the Bushmen.

10.Epupa Falls
Epupa Falls - a waterfall on the Kunene River. The height of 37 m, width of 500 m. The waterfall is one of the main attractions of Angola and Namibia. To visit the falls are special tours that offer to enjoy the greenery, to see crocodiles, native birds, baobabs, as well as camping and rafting.
