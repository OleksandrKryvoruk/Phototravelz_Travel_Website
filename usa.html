1.New York
New York is the city with the largest population in the United States. The city is the center of international trade, politics, entertainment and culture. The city has a collection of museums, art galleries, theaters, international organizations and corporations, as well as stock exchanges. The city is also home to the United Nations. The basis of modern architecture of New York is the skyscrapers - houses more than 150 m high. It is worth visiting: the Museum of Modern Art, the American Museum of Natural History, the Metropolitan Museum, the New York Symphony Orchestra, the New York Philharmonic Orchestra, the Metropolitan Opera, the Broadway Theater, The Statue of Liberty, Empire State Building, Ellis Island, Central Park, Rockefeller Center, Times Square, Bronx Zoo, New York Botanical Garden, Fifth and Madison Avenue.

3.Las Vegas
Las Vegas is a city in the western US, Nevada. The center of tourism and games of national importance. Known as the "city of vices", which has a casino, discos, slot machines. Major casinos and hotels are located in the city center along Fremont Street and Las Vegas Boulevard, the city's main street, the lively part of which is called the Las Vegas Strip or simply the Strip. In addition to the casino, the city is also famous for the fact that you can marry there without a preliminary announcement, in 5 minutes. Tallest Buildings: Stratosphere Tower - 350 meters, the Sphinx in the casino, "Luxor», Fontainebleau Resort Las Vegas - 224 m, The Palazzo - 196 m, Encore - 192 m, Trump International Hotel & Tower - Tower One -190 m.

4.Miami
Miami - a city on the Atlantic coast of Florida, USA. The city's most famous area of Miami Beach - one of the most luxurious places in the world with a lot of nightlife, historic sites, shops and beaches. The city has a number of museums: the Miami Art Museum, Frost Art Museum, Miami Children's Museum, the Science Museum. The city hosts festivals: Calle Ocho, Miami Carnival, Ultra Music Festival. Miami has many canals, rivers where you can go fishing and boating. Also worth visiting parks Bicentennial Park, Tropical Park, Peacock Park, Zoo Miami, Miami Seaquarium, Fairchild Botanical Gardens and Coral Castle.

5.Chicago
Chicago - the third largest city in the United States. The main attractions include: Willis Tower (the second tallest US building height of 443 m.), Skyscrapers Quarter, Old Town, Gold Coast (accumulation of old beautiful mansions), sculpture by Pablo Picasso, Water Tower, Chicago Yacht Club. Also, the city has more than 30 beaches, a zoo, several bird sanctuaries, fountains and a botanical garden. The city has many museums including: Adler Planetarium, Field Museum of Natural History, Museum of Science and Industry, Museum of Architecture and Design of the city, the Museum of Modern Art. Also, the city has the status of the US theater capital.

6.San Francisco
San Francisco - city in California, USA. The city has many museums: the Museum of Modern Art, Asian Art Museum, Science Museum, Exploratorium, the UFO Museum, Walt Disney Museum, the Museum of Russian Culture, the Museum of Bigfoot and the Loch Ness monster, the Wax Museum. The city zoo is located next to Lake Merced and contains more than 250 species of animals, including 40 endangered species. The city hosts a number of events: Fair Folsom Street (September), Chinese New Year (February), the rave festival Lavfest (October), the San Francisco Marathon, Fireworks show (May).

7.Walt Disney World
Walt Disney World - the largest and most visited entertainment center in the world, covers an area of ​​121.7 km2. It consists of four theme parks, two water parks, 23 theme hotels, two spa resorts, fitness centers and other recreational facilities. A whole bus fleet running Disney, with the brand "Disney Transport", is available to visitors for free. Located south-west of the city of Orlando, Florida, USA. The construction of Disney World turned Orlando from a low-profile village into a thriving tourist center. Every year about 20 million people visit the park.

8.Universal Studios Hollywood
Universal Studios Hollywood is a film studio and theme park located in the Hollywood area in Los Angeles, California, USA. It is one of Hollywood's oldest and most famous film studios. The main attraction of the park is the Studio Tour. During the tour, cars are passing a few shooting. 9.Hollywood
Hollywood is a district of Los Angeles, USA. The area is associated with the cinema industry, because there are a lot of film studios, also here live many actors. In the district there is an alley of glory, where more than 2500 stars with names of celebrities are laid out. Many historical Hollywood theaters are used as a scene and concert scenes for premieres of movies, as well as a place to reward the American Film Academy. This is a popular place for nightlife and tourism. Local Attractions: Capitol Records, Hollywood Roosevelt Hotel, Kodak Theater, Hollywood Sign, Hollywood Walk of Fame.

10.The Statue of Liberty
The Statue of Liberty is a national monument located on Liberty Island, New York, USA. The statue was built of thin sheets of copper minted in wooden forms. Formed sheets were later installed on a steel frame. Since its opening, the statue served as a navigational reference point and was used as a beacon. Usually a statue is open to visitors who arrive by ferry. From the crown, which you can climb the stairs, opens a vast view of the New York harbor. In the museum, located in the pedestal is an exhibition of the history of the statue. The height of 93 m. The monument was opened in 1886. The statue is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
