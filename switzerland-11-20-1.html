Jungfrau - mountain with a height of 4158 meters the highest peak in the mountain range of the same name in the Bern region in the Swiss Alps, as well as the third highest peak in the Bernese Oberland. 15.Rhine Falls
Rhine Falls - a waterfall on the Rhine River in the Swiss canton of Schaffhausen, near the town of Neuhausen am Rheinfall. The Rhine waterfall is considered to be the largest flat waterfall in Europe. The height of the waterfall is 23 m, the width is 150 m. The average water discharge in winter is 250 m3 / s, in the summer - 700 m3 / s. The Rhine Falls is a popular tourist destination. At the waterfall are several viewing areas. The main observation deck is on a cliff in the center of the waterfall, where you can get on a tourist boat. Boat mooring is located at the castle of Wörth from below down the waterfall. Also tourists can visit the castle Laufen located on the other shore.

17.Chillon Castle
Chillon Castle is located on the coast of Leman in Veit, Switzerland. The elongated shape, the castle: 110 meters long and 50 meters wide, with a tower 25 meters high. Since the end of 18 - th century, the castle attracts romantics. Castle inspires poets from around the world, among them Jean-Jacques Rousseau, Victor Hugo, Alexandre Dumas, Lord Byron. The monument receives more than 300 000 visitors per year.

20.Mount Pilatus
Mount Pilatus - a mountain in Switzerland. Located to the southwest of the city of Lucerne. Throughout the year, with the Pilatus can be viewed on the natural panorama of the cantons. Mountain rises to an altitude of 1585 meters above Lake Lucerne and the mountain peak reaches a height of 2128 m above sea level. On the mountain is the cable car and at the top there is a tourist complex. During the summer, you can take part in the popular tourist route Golden Round Trip, which includes a boat cruise from Lucerne across Lake Lucerne, it is also a toboggan run.
