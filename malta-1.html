Top 10 places for travel & vacation in Malta.

EUROPE > South Europe
1.Valletta
Valletta - Malta's capital city, is located on the island of Malta, on the Mediterranean coast. Tourist and shopping center. The ancient center of the Hospitallers (Knights of Malta) - kept their palaces. Fort St. Elma was one of the main defensive forts Valletta. Now the fort is a military museum. The city gates are located on Freedom Square, where the right is the tower of St. James Cavalier, for it is a knightly farmstead Auberge de Castille, which is the residence of the Prime Minister of Malta. Opposite Liberty Square is the Palazzo Ferrero - one of the most beautiful palaces built in the style of Italian classics, which previously housed the armory of knights. A little further you can see the building of the Royal Opera. Building Auberge de Provence, built in 1574 during the English rule, was the center of Malta's public life and called the Union Club. Now here is the National Museum of Archaeology. In Valletta is Malta's National Library. Also here is the first Anglican cathedral on the Island - St. Paul's Cathedral. Another Valletta Cathedral - Cathedral of St. John. Among the other attractions worth mentioning Casa Rocca Piccola, one of the others is not rebuilt palaces Malta and the Jesuit Church, one of the oldest churches in the city.

2.Comino
Comino - an island in the Mediterranean Sea, part of the Maltese archipelago. In the west, near the small island of Comino it is Cominotto, and between them is known among tourists Blue Lagoon. The extreme northern point - Cape Emir, very Southern - Cape Ras Ile Irkika. On the last set lighthouse. The permanent population of the island is 4 people, including a priest, a police officer and overseer of the lighthouse. In the southwest, in the bay of Santa Maria, is a tower of Santa Maria, built in 1618. The island is popular with tourists and movie producers. On the island were shooting films such as Troy, The Count of Monte Cristo (used to lock the tower of Santa Maria), Gone. The only hotel on the island of Comino Hotel is located on the north of the island and is only valid from April to October.

3.Popeye Village
Popeye Village - amusement park in Malta in the village of Mellieha, in the Bay Anchor Bay. Today the village is open all year round for tourists as a museum and family entertainment complex, is one of the tourist attractions of Malta. For tourists organized a 15-minute boat trips around the bay, where you can take pictures and see the village from the sea. The bottom has a cinema complex, which displays every hour projections on the film and its creation. The resort also organizes shows, for example: puppet shows.

Dingli Cliffs is a small village in the west of Malta. The village is located in the highest part of Malta. One of the oldest villages in Malta. This village is known mainly by steep cliffs, standing by the sea and a small island within Filfla. In Dingli You can also find great Buskett gardens.

6.Marsaxlokk
Marsaxlokk - fishing village in the south-eastern region of Malta. It is a big fair, which takes place every Sunday throughout the village, and there are also tourist fairs that take place throughout the week. Locals are known as Xlukkajri and has long been a fisherman. In Marsaxlokk is only preserved in Malta bashta- redoubt Vendome tower previously existed redoubt del Fango. Around the bay of Marsaxlokk in different periods of the network of fortifications was built for its defense against attacks. First between 1610 and 1611 years was built by Fort St Lucian Tower .After her territory Marsaxlokk in 1659 built two towers Redin and Delimara. The work Marsaxlokk fish restaurants, in addition, the flow of tourists here has led to the emergence of street vendors and souvenir sellers, who were the first, who contributed to the development of the local tourism industry.

7.Gozo
Gozo - is a small island in the Mediterranean Sea, Malta. Gozo is sparsely populated and is known for its picturesque hills. On the island there are a number of objects included in the list of the National list of cultural values: Mgarr Tower IX-Xini, Xlendi Tower, Dwejra Tower, Rotunda in Sheukia, Basilica of the Annunciation of the Blessed Virgin Mary.
