The castle was built in the shape of a rectangle with sides of 75 and 105 meters, the walls 15 meters high and consists of eight towers 45 meters in height. Warrick is the best medieval castle in the country. The castle is known for one of the most important European collections of arms and armor, along with paintings by masters such as Rubens and Van Dyck.

29.Dover Castle
Dover Castle - a medieval castle in the town of Dover, in the English county of Kent. The castle is often called or referred to as "the key to England ', referring to its extreme importance for the defense of the country during the time of its existence. Dover Castle - the largest of the castles of England. The castle is protected by the state as a historic building of national importance.
