Top 20 places for travel & vacation in Chile. 11-20

SOUTH AMERICA
01-10 11-20
11.Torres del Paine
Torres del Paine National Park is one of Chile's largest and most important parks. About 75% of park visitors come from Europe. In the park you can see mountains under 3000 m, rivers, valleys, lakes and glaciers. In 2013, the park won the eighth wonder of the world award. Every year the park is visited by more than 140 000 tourists, and National Geographic has set the park to the 5 most beautiful place of the planet. Also in the park is a huge number of unique plants and animals.

12.San Pedro de Atacama
San Pedro de Atacama - settlement in Chile. Located near the Atacama desert and the great salt plains of Salar de Atacama and the beautiful Moon Valley. In the area of ​​the village live 4 species of flamingo. There is also a 17th century church of San Pedro, a settlement in the north of the fortress pukará de quitor. In the south, there is the ancient settlement of Chile - tulor.

13.Pisco Elqui
Pisco Elqui is a Chilean village inside the Elqui Valley, 1300 m above sea level. The Elqui Valley is one of the most visited places in the northern part of the country. The valley is considered an energy place, the center of UFO and esoteric. It is worth visiting the Gabriela Mistral Museum, the Mamalluca Observatory. Historically, in the 16th century, the first production of pisco was recorded here. Near the village is the oldest distillery Pisco Elki, Los Nikos. In addition, the unique clear sky offers the best conditions for visiting one of the many observatories in the region that are known all over the world.
Where to stay Weather Things to do in Pisco Elqui: Food, Wine & Nightlife Tours & Sightseeing
 

14.Valle de la Luna
Valle de la Luna is a desolate tourist attraction in the Los Flamencos Nature Reserve, Chile. The desert is located in a depression surrounded by a 500 m high mountain range of the Cordillera de la Sal, near the city of San Pedro de Atacama. The desert is located on a thick ball of halite (up to 10 m) above which there are layers of sand and gravel. Under the influence of salt erosion, many unique figures were formed here, which can be seen on hills, mountains and rocks. When the sun falls on the desert, it changes color from gold to purple.
Where to stay Weather Things to do in Moon Valley: Tours & Sightseeing
 

15.Los Pingüinos Natural Monument
Los Pingüinos Natural Monument - protected wildlife of Chile. It covers 97 hectares, of which 85 correspond to the island of Magdalena and 12 to the island of Marta. This is one of the most important penguin reserves in southern Chile. Between 2001 and 2002, there were 60,000 pairs of Magellanic penguins at the census site. In addition, there are other species, such as cormorants, gulls and sea lions. Monumento nacional los pinguinos is open from October to March.
Where to stay Weather Things to do in Los Pingüinos Natural Monument: Tours & Sightseeing Transfers & Ground Transport
 

16.La Moneda Palace
Palace "La Moneda" - residence of the President of Chile in the country's capital. Construction of the building began in 1784. Buildings include the Blue Room - the working office of the President, the Government Conference Hall, the Cabinet of the Minister of Internal Affairs, the Cabinet of Salvador Allende, White Hall. In 2003, President Ricardo Lagos opened the palace for tourists. In 2006, in front of its southern facade appeared the Citizens Square, on which the cultural center of the palace was erected and a monument to President Arturo Alessandri was erected.

17.Villarrica
Villarrica is an active volcano with a height of 1575 m in the Villarrica National Park, Chile. From July to September, there is a ski resort, helicopter tours are also popular. One of the last eruptions was in 2015, the volcano itself was formed more than 3,500 years ago. There are more than 30 institutions that offer a visit to the volcano, its caves and in winter time skiing.
Where to stay Weather Things to do in Villarrica: Food, Wine & Nightlife Tours & Sightseeing

18.Osorno
Osorno - a volcano in height 2,652 m near the lake Llanquihue in Chile. It is located in the Vicente-Pérez-Rosales National Park, the oldest national park in Chile, founded in 1926. The exterior looks like Mount Fuji. The most active volcano of the Chilean Andes. The last eruption was back in 1869. Also near the volcano there are Petrohue waterfalls and a ski resort. Up to a height of 1300 m you can drive along a new asphalt road to a mountain hut. To the east of the volcano is Lake Lago-Todos-los Santos with its island Isla Margarita.
Where to stay Weather Things to do in Osorno: Day Trips & Excursions Multi-day & Extended Tours Outdoor Activities Private & Custom Tours Shore Excursions
 

19.General Carrera Lake
Lake Buenos Aires or General Carrera Lake - a lake on the border of Argentina and Chile. The largest lake in Chile. The maximum depth of 586 m. It has a glacial origin. In the lake are found salmon and trout. Lake also has big beautiful marble caves - this is its main uniqueness. The most famous tourist attraction is the "Marble Cathedral", islets in the middle of the lake, consisting of white and ivory rocks. They are also visited by fans of sport fishing, because of the abundance of trout and other salmon.
Where to stay Weather
 

20.Cajon del Maipo
Cajón del Maipo - canyon in the Andes. It covers the upper part of the Maipo River basin. Canyon is an hour's drive from Chile's capital Santiago de Chile and is a popular center for ecotourism in the country. Here you can do trekking, horse riding, riding bicycles, rafts, boats, skis and snowboarding. It is worth visiting the El Morado Nature Monument, the thermal baths of Banos Morales, El Yeso.
