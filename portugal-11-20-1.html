13.Barcelos
Barcelos - a city in northern Portugal. Barcelos has eleven national monuments and eight buildings of public interest. Architectural attractions: Castrejo, Vila - Cova, Necropolis Mondim - Panque, Gil Vicente Theatre, Museum of Ceramics, Castle of Faria, Wall of Barcelos, Palace of the Counts of Barcelos, Village Top Tower. Museums: City Art Gallery, Archaeological Museum of Barcelos (palace ruins), the Pottery Museum, the Ethnographic Museum Chavão, Regional and Ethnographic Museum of Alvito. The city has five hiking trails. Festivals in the city: Barcelos Theatre Festival - in September and October, Art & Tur - International Tourism Film Festival, fashion Barcelos, Sweet exposure Folar Easter, Subscuta - Alternative music festival.
