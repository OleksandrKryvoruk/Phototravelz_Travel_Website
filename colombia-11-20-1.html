Top 20 places for travel & vacation in Colombia. 11-20

SOUTH AMERICA

11.Parque Natural Chicaque
Natural Park Chicaque - a fleet of 7 types of forests, which are located on 300 hectares, Colombia. The reserve is home to over 214 species of birds and 30 species of mammals. A popular place for eco-tourism. The place has a well-developed infrastructure: Environmental Centre, camping, shops, restaurant, hostel and horse paddock. It is worth to see and visit the Eagle Peak (rock with a high content of carbon), Falls (70 m high), Colonial trail Forest Robles (trees over 300 years old). In the south-east of the park live a lot of butterflies.

12.Riosucio
Riosucio (Caldas) - Town carnival and festival. Located in the west of the department of Caldas, Colombia. The city retained the ancient culture of the Colombians. The city has many artistic groups. In the city every two years, a carnival, which is considered an intangible cultural heritage of Colombia. Carnival combines African, European and indigenous culture. Also in the city of festivals Fiestas patronales de la Virgen de la Candelaria (in late January), Fiestas de San Lorenzo, Fiestas del Guarapo.
Where to stay Weather
 

13.Pianguita
Pianguita Beach - beach near the town of Bonaventure, Colombia. Quiet, peaceful place. Places near the beach are inexpensive. There are 3 meals a day. The kitchen is very tasty. In pianguita, you can feel safe because the army is always on the beach. If you like, you can go around the city to other beaches that are more lonely, almost without tourists, if you like to have privacy.
Where to stay Weather
 

14.Chocontá
Chocontá or Leal y Noble Villa de Santiago de Chocontá, Colombia. The city is located in the north-east of Bogota. Located at an altitude of 2689 m above sea level. The main attraction of the region is its nature is a lot of mountains, ridges, hills, rivers, dams, are striking in their beauty. The city hosts festivals: Festival of dance in pairs (March), birthday of the city and the Festival de Raigambres y Tradiciones Campesinas (September), fairs and local festivals (November), the Day of the Virgin of Our Lady (October). Worth visiting Hot Springs Via Macheta, Hot Springs Vereda Boqueron, Capilla del Santo Cristo Calvary, the Municipal Palace, the House of Culture.

15.Biblioteca Pública Virgilio Barco
Virgilio Barco Public Library - Library in the south of Bogotá, Colombia. The library is surrounded by gardens, waterways, ponds and channels, which are bike paths and restaurants. The building has a circular shape with a lot of terraces, stairs, ramps. In the library of more than 150 000 volumes. Even if you are not interested in the library, the building itself, you have to visit - it is an architectural masterpiece.

16.Parque Metropolitano Simón Bolívar
Simon Bolivar Park - the largest and most important park of Bogotá, Colombia. The park area of ​​400 hectares. The main part of the park is 113 hectares and includes the lake, playground, room for up to 80,000 people, where the Theatre Festival Iberoamericano. The second part of the park Los Novios includes playgrounds, soccer fields and tennis, motocross station and a lake with pedal boats and kayaks. The third part of Salitre Park consists of amusement, amusement park and Ferris wheel. Also worth a visit Children's Museum, Palacio - de - los - Deportes, Watersports Complex (4 pools), High Performance Center, Virgilio Barco Library, the Botanical Garden José Celestino Mutis (has more than 5,000 orchids), Golf Course.

17.Dapa
Dapa - tourist region in the Valle del - Cauca, Colombia. It has a pleasant mild climate and is situated at an altitude of 1,000 to 2,000 meters above sea level. The city offers all the best of Colombian and Argentine cuisine. Also popular mountain biking, hiking, environmental workshops, bird watching, horseback riding. The nature of the region's striking beauty, there are many orchids, eucalyptus and tropical birds.
Where to stay Weather
 

18.San Diego
Río Don Diego - a river in the north of Colombia. The river runs near one of the oldest cities in Colombia Santa Marta. River flows through the beautiful green forests, has clean water and is home to hundreds of species of animals. Locals spend forest excursions, where you can see beautiful birds, butterflies and monkeys. There is also a small archaeological museum.
Where to stay Weather
 

19.Envigado
Envigado - a town in southern Aburrá Valley, Colombia. A popular place for eco-tourism. One of the main attractions is the ecological park El Salado. It is worth visiting the Church of Santa - Gertrudis (1897), the Church of San - Jose (1947), House of Culture "Miguel Uribe Restrepo", Otraparte House - Museum, Parque La Morena, Leisure park, Municipal Theatre, Gerontology Atarde-Ser, Angel Falls.

20.Castillo de San Felipe de Barajas
Castillo San -Felipe - de - Barajas - Fortress in Cartagena, Colombia. The fortress was built in 1657, and since 1984 part of the UNESCO World Heritage List. In the Middle Ages the fortress was constantly attacks on the British and French, so it is often restored. The fortress is located on a hill 25 meters, and its dimensions are 300 by 100 meters. Now the castle is overgrown by tropical forest and has become a place of cultural events.
