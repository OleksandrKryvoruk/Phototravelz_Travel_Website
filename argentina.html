1.Buenos Aires
Buenos Aires - capital of Argentina on the bay of Rio de la Plata. The city has a variety of historical and modern architecture. You can find these architectural styles: Gothic, neo-gothic, neoclassics, Art Nouveau and Art Deco. The city has 640 parks that affect diversity of flora. For cultural sites include the Museum of Fine Arts Eduardo Sivori Museum, Casa Rosada, Argentina's National History Museum, National Museum of Fine Arts, National Museum of Decorative Arts, Teatro Colón, Teatro General San Martín, Teatro de la Ribera, Centro Cultural General San Martin (of the city more than 300 theaters). Festivals: tango festival (August), Festival of Independent Cinema, and many others.

2.Córdoba
 By Fernando Hidalgo CC2
Córdoba is a city in Argentina, the capital of the province of Córdoba. The city is located in the central region of Argentina, near the geographical center of the country. In the center of Cordoba, colonial buildings have been preserved, attracting a large number of tourists. In 2000, the Jesuit Quarter (an ensemble of buildings, including the residence of the prelate, the church, the university, schools and four country missions) became a UNESCO World Heritage Site. In the city there is a botanical garden and a zoo, in which live more than a thousand animals. Worth visiting: Theater of San Martín, Municipal Theater of Comedy, Royal Theater, Museum of Fine Arts, Juan de Tejeda Museum of Religious Art, Museum of Natural Sciences, Iberoamerican Crafts Museum.

3.Rosario
By Nils Wedding CC2
Rosario is a city in the province of Santa Fe, the third largest city in Argentina. The main tourist attraction is the Banner Monument, which is built on the site where the flag of Argentina was first raised. In Rosario, many buildings of colonial architecture, as well as houses in the style of art deco and modernism, have been preserved. In Rosario there are several cultural centers and about 30 theaters, the most famous of which is El Cirkulo. Also in the city there are many cinemas and cinemas, there is a film school and film studio. 2009 in the city was opened a casino, which is considered the largest in Latin America. The following events are held annually in the city: a jazz festival in July, a sculpture festival in September, and a film festival in November.

4.Salta
Salta is a city in the north-west of Argentina, the capital of the province of Salta. It is located in the east of the Andean mountain range in the Lerma valley at an altitude of 1187 meters above sea level. Outstanding architectural monuments of the city: The municipality is the oldest building of the city, the Cathedral, the Cathedral. The Archaeological High Mountain Museum is also one of the most important tourist attractions of Salta. It exhibits the culture of the Incas and the mummies of Ljullyaylako. Other attractions of the city are the Museum of Fine Arts and the City Museum, the Legislative Assembly and the House Museum of Guumes. From the city of Salta begins the path of the tourist beyond the clouds Trains, which rides the Altiplano and the slopes of the Andes to the town of San Antonio de los Cobres.

5.Mendoza
Mendoza - city and capital of the homonymous province of Argentina. It has the informal name a city of sun and good wine. Near the town is Mount Aconcagua, the capital of Chile, Santiago. The city is located in an oasis where the vines grow. Also near the city is one of the most modern ski resorts in the country. It is worth visiting the church of Saint Francis ruins, park them. 6.Bariloche
San Carlos de Bariloche is a town at the foot of the Andes, Argentina. The city is surrounded by mountains, lakes and is a popular holiday destination. The city has several ski resorts, as well as popular water sports, mountaineering and trekking. Near the town there is Mount Cerro Catedral - one of the centers of the country's skiing. The city is located near the national parks Nahuel-Uapi (Lake of the same name) and Los Arrananes (Arranian Forest), which annually attract local and foreign tourists. In winter, Bariloche hosts two festivals: the National Snow Festival and the Festival of Communities.

7.Glaciar Perito Moreno
Perito Moreno Glacier is a glacier in the Los Glyacious National Park in the southwest of the Argentine province of Santa Cruz. The glacier is located 78 km from the village of El-Calafate, which can be reached by airplane. The glacial plateau of Patagonia has the 3rd largest freshwater reserve on Earth. The width of the glacier is 5 km, the height is 60 m, the depth is up to 700 m and the speed of movement is 2 m per day. In recent years, guided tours on ice have become increasingly popular. There are 2 options: "mini-tracking", consisting of a half-hour walk and "big ice", which lasts about five hours.

Santa Teresita is a city in the province of Buenos Aires in Argentina. One of the best resorts in Argentina. In this city you can enjoy the sea, sand, sports such as windsurfing or surfing, strolling through its green spaces or its tree-lined streets, shopping in the center or enjoying the night life in several clubs. The city has a good climate, many beaches, windsurfing, golf, many night clubs, hotels and a large pier for fishing.

9.Iguazu Falls
Iguazu Falls - a complex of 275 waterfalls on the river of the same name on the border of Argentina and Brazil. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Height 82 m, width 2700 m. Also included in the list of seven natural wonders of the world. The waterfall began to form 130 million years ago. Near the waterfall, many hotels, campsites, trails and theaters. Iguazu Falls is one of the most visited places in South America. Every year, there are 1.5-2 million visitors.

10.Mar del Plata
Mar del Plata is a resort town and port on the Argentinian coast. Located in the south-east of the province of Buenos Aires. The sea coast of the city extends for more than 28 km. In 2006, the city was visited by more than 7 million tourists. Mar del Plata has a well-developed structure of roads, hotels, restaurants and other tourist attractions. Every year the city hosts an international film festival. For night entertainment in it there are more than 150 institutions, such as bars, discos, pubs, casinos and others.
